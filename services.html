How Every Retainer Term Works

Each monthly term is structured the same way — strategy first, then production. Content doesn't get written without a strategic reason behind it.

Strategy Opens Every Term

Each month begins with a fresh roadmap, updated keyword research, and search intent mapping. The strategy layer informs every piece of content produced that month — the two are inseparable.

Flexible Content Scope

Production is drawn from a defined service menu agreed upfront — new AEO pages, revisions, metadata, social copy, community seeding, and more. Scope is locked before the term begins so there are no surprises mid-month.

Measured and Reported Monthly

AI citation share, SERP feature acquisition, ranking movement, and organic traffic are tracked every term. You always know what changed, why, and what the next move is.

Built to Compound

Content authority, entity signals, and structured data accumulate over time. Each term's work builds directly on the last — which is why the results curve steepens after month three, not flattens.
